Guild Wars 2

Guild wars 2 is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) developed by ArenaNet and published by NCsoft. It is set in the fantasty world of Tyria, where the player takes on the role of a hero and battles against enemies, complete quests, and participates in large-scale events with other players. The game features a dynamic, story-driven world that changes based on player choices, and offers a wide variety of professions and playstyles to choose from.
